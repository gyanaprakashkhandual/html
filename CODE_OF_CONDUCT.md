# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making this project a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free space for everyone — regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

---

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Welcome beginners and help them grow
- Give and receive feedback graciously
- Focus on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent

---

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to the maintainer at **[your email or GitHub profile]**. All reports will be reviewed and handled with discretion.

Maintainers reserve the right to remove contributions or ban participants who violate this code.
